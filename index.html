Active hypothesis · v0 · the overhead view

When AI collapses the cost of building, coherence becomes the scarce resource.

Parallax is a viewpoint name for the overhead view of a coherence stack — 21 composable products and 17 frameworks that compose along one loop (Notice → Spec → Execute → Observe), governed by the Intent operating model. It is not a product, not a container, and not a methodology book. It is the surface from which the relationships between the parts become legible — and challengeable.

Parallax: you resolve the depth of a thing by viewing it from separated vantage points. The name does two jobs — depth resolves with altitude separation, and depth is inferred from displacement across viewpoints. That is exactly what this system does to its own work.

Why it exists

AI changed the cost curve, not the destination.

Everything downstream of the model is now cheap — the implementation, the typing, the boilerplate. Everything upstream of the model — the noticing, the framing, the deciding — is where the leverage moved. Teams that keep optimizing for the old constraint (coordinating human labor through tickets and sprints) build overhead around the wrong bottleneck.

The founding reframe is one move: from a coordination tax to a coordination investment. Agile ceremony evaporates after each sprint — it is paid down, repeatedly. Coherence artifacts compound — they are paid forward, once. That single shift is the argument the discipline rests on.

Coordination tax
Standups, story points, sprint rituals. Re-paid every cycle. Evaporates on completion. Scales with headcount.
→
Coordination investment
Compiled knowledge, specs-as-contracts, preserved dissent, traceable decisions. Paid forward once. Compounds.
What Parallax is

A viewpoint, not a container.

Parallax sits above the stack as the shareable narrative surface. It composes the overhead view from the products' state — it does not own or contain them (sibling-over-parent-child is the architectural law here).

It is

  • The overhead view — altitude model + the relationships across the stack
  • A surface that publishes from the products below, never wraps them
  • Brand + narrative architecture — a way to read the system, not run it
  • An honest hypothesis under pressure test

It is not

  • The discipline — that is Coherence Engineering, held internal until the gates open
  • The execution framework — that is Intent, at a different pipeline position
  • A container or parent of the other products
  • A category — not yet. It earns the name by what it produces, not what it claims

Where it's unproven

This is a hypothesis. Here is where to swing.

The honest standing of the claim

This is not yet a category; it is a hypothesis under pressure test. The discipline name — Coherence Engineering — is held internal on purpose, and only earns public use once three external-articulation gates clear. Until then the work speaks in its sibling vocabulary: prompt, context, and harness engineering.

And the honest market: the serviceable addressable audience today is roughly one — me. That is exactly why this surface exists. A worldview cannot advance past theory, or past an audience of its author, unless its view is expressable, navigable, and challengeable. So: navigate it, and challenge it.

Gate 1 · open The Voices-judged coherence-debt register resolves to zero.
Gate 2 · open The CON-COH machine-assertable contracts pass against the corpus.

The sharpest open question, stated plainly for the reader most likely to ask it: is the surface area too large? Twenty-one products is either coherence operationalized — or sprawl with a viewpoint name on top. If you'd implode part of it, that's the conversation this page is for.

Where Parallax stands — and how to go deeper

Against what alternative.

Dunford's question: what are the real alternatives a collaborator has today, and why does none of them hold this position?

Fragmented consulting frameworks — disconnected playbooks for discovery, delivery, strategy. No altitude model, no throughline between them.
Agile transformation services — execution-posture work (how we work) that rarely touches the altitude problem (are we even at the right altitude?).
Narrative strategy firms — they work on how you describe the work, not on the coherence of the work itself.

None holds altitude + coherence + narrative simultaneously. That intersection is the position — claimed as a hypothesis, not yet as a category.
